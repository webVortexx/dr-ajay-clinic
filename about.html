About the doctor

Dr Ajay Prajapati has built Dr Ajay Homeopathic Clinic in Garha, Jabalpur around a simple idea: no two patients are the same, so no two treatments should be either. He takes time to understand not just your symptoms, but your routine, temperament and history — because in homeopathy, those details are what point to the right remedy.

Patients of all ages visit the clinic, from children with recurring coughs and colds to adults managing long-standing skin, digestive, joint and hormonal complaints. Whether the concern is sudden or something you've lived with for years, the aim is the same: gentle, individually chosen care.

Qualifications & registration

BHMS (2019, RDVV Jabalpur) and MD (2023, Madhya Pradesh Medical Science University). Registered with the Madhya Pradesh State Homeopathy Council (Reg. No. 25020, since 2019) and a member of the Homeopathic Medical Association of India (HMAI). Consultations are offered in English and Hindi.

His approach

Homeopathy treats the person, not only the disease. That means looking at how a complaint actually shows up in your life — when it flares, what makes it better or worse, and how it affects your sleep, energy and mood. From there, a single well-matched remedy is chosen and adjusted over follow-up visits as you improve.

You'll always leave with a clear plan: what to take, how often, and when to come back.
